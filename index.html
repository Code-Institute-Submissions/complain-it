Complain It

Complain a local tradesman if the job he/she did is not good enough, so other people can rely on your experience and they can avoid unnecessary hustle.

How it works ?

accessibility

Find your tradesman

At first it is advised to check in 'Search for complained job' in the menu section. If you find your desired tradesman you can judge yourself if the job he/she has done is satisfactory for YOU.

Complain the tradesman

If you wish to complain a tradesman simply use 'Complain a job' from navigation bar, then follow the guide how to upload a job you are not happy about.

Spread the Word!

If you are satisfied using our web application, simply spread the word about it to your friends, family or colleagues so they can avoid dissapointments in the future.



Our Target

We are a non-profit organisation that helps people with selecting proper tradesman for a certain job. It allows user to see other job whenever they are good or bad. Our application will always be for FREE.
